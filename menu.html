Our top 5 signature dishes.

Spring Rolls

Spring rolls are a popular appetizer or snack, consisting of a thin pastry wrapper filled with a variety of ingredients like vegetables, meat, or seafood. They can be either fried until crispy or served fresh with the filling wrapped in rice paper

Polish Mushroom Soup

The soup is often prepared with a rich broth made from meat or vegetable stock, onions, garlic, carrots, and sometimes potatoes. It is seasoned with herbs like bay leaves and allspice, and finished with a touch of cream or sour cream to add richness.

Bruschetta met scampi & avocado

Bruschetta with Scampi & Avocado is a flavorful appetizer featuring crispy toasted bread topped with creamy mashed avocado and succulent, lemon-seasoned shrimp. Garnished with fresh herbs and a drizzle of olive oil.

Deviled Eggs

Deviled eggs are a classic appetizer made from hard-boiled eggs, where the yolks are removed, mixed with ingredients like mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, and spices, then spooned or piped back into the egg whites.

Fruit Skewers

Fruit skewers are a fun and refreshing snack or dessert made by threading a variety of fresh, bite-sized fruits onto skewers. Common fruits used include strawberries, grapes, pineapple, melon, and kiwi, but any fruit can be added.
